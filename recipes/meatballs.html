Meatballs

For many people their favourite go-to food.
Usually bought oven ready from the store this time deliciously home-made!

Ingredients

  • Minced meat: 500g(porc-veal)
  • Ricotta
  • Mozarella slices
  • Tomatoes: 1kg
  • Flour: 400g
  • Salt
  • Pepper

How to make lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to layer lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce

How to cook lasagna


The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
